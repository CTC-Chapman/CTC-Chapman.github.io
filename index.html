Our Purpose

To promote social good by creating custom, sustainable software for nonprofit organizations.

Our goal as a club is to partner with nonprofits in order to provide well designed integrated websites for them. Many nonprofits are bound in their reach and scope of technology, so we're here to help execute their goals of exapnidng their reach.

We are commited to helping those in need and putting ourselves out there. We want to make a change to help for the better and with our technology and expertise we can help to make these changes.
