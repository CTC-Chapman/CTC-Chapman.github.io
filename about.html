ABOUT CODE THE CHANGE

Code The Change is a student-run organization based at Chapman University in Orange, California. Our mission is to utilize our diverse skill sets to develop impactful websites for charitable and nonprofit organizations. We collaborate with a diverse range of students from all different majors to build, design, and promote websites aimed at driving a positive change in our communities. Our goal is to develop both educational and professional skills through these meaningful projects.
